Movie Colors: Multiple Exposures

A multiple exposure is formed by taking multiple pictures and combining them into a single image:

The images below are a result of combining every frame of a movie into a single image.

If you look closely, you'll see a pattern similar to screen burn-in formed by common elements of the movie.
